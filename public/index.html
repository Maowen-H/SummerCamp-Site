Program Overview

Our goal is to engage diverse, motivated, and talented undergraduate and graduate students around the nation, and train them to adopt cybersecurity knowledge and skills in the deployment of large language models into advanced cyberinfrastructure systems.

Tutorials and Interactive Hands-on Labs

Identification, analysis, and mitigation of diverse attack vectors targeting LLM-empowered cyberinfrastructure systems.

Seminars & Talks

Share cutting-edge LLM security research and provide professional career training.

Research Lab Tours

Campus tour of the research facilities and institutes, and interact with researchers.
